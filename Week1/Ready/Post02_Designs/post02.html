5 red flags
in your next tech interview.
Spot these before you sign. Senior devs, this is for you.
Swipe →

Red flag 01
1
"Why is this role open?"
If they stumble, dodge, or give a five-minute answer full of words like "restructure" — that's the job talking.
2 / 5
Red flag 02
2
A 20+ hour take-home.
That's a full weekend, unpaid, to audition for a job they may not give you. Respectable companies test differently.
3 / 5
Red flag 03
3
"You'll report to the team."
Nobody reports to a team. If they can't name your manager, the org chart is chaos.
4 / 5
Red flag 04
4
No one mentions engineering culture.
No talk of code review, on-call, or planning cadence means they don't have any of it yet.
5 / 5
Red flag 05
5
"We'll discuss salary at offer."
No band, no range, no comfort with the conversation. Seniors walk. You should too.
